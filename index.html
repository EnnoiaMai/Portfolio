About Me

My name is Thuc Nguyen. I graduated from UCSD in 2017 with a Human Biology major. During the years as an undergraduate, I grew a strong interest in Computer Science and started learning how to program offhand. I looked into C++ as my first programming language to gain some fundamental understanding of how to code. However, I have recently completed a post-baccalaureate program for Web and Mobile Application Development at SDSU. Despite my lack of a C.S degree, I am still motivated to pursue this field. This is a portfolio of all the work I've done and am able to show.
